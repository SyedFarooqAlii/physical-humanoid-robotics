Welcome to the comprehensive curriculum on Physical AI & Humanoid Robotics! This educational resource is designed to guide you through the fascinating world of humanoid robots, combining artificial intelligence with advanced robotics systems.

This curriculum covers four essential modules:

- **Module 1**: The Robotic Nervous System (ROS 2) - Learn about communication and control systems
- **Module 2**: The Digital Twin (Gazebo & Unity) - Explore simulation environments
- **Module 3**: The AI-Robot Brain (NVIDIA Isaac) - Understand AI integration
- **Module 4**: Vision-Language-Action (VLA) - Master multimodal AI systems
